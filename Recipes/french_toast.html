French Toast

French toast is a classic dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. commonly served as a sweet dish with sugar and cinnamon but can be served with banana or other fruits for a healthy snack

Ingriedients: 4 Servings

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices bread

Instructions

  1. eat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.

  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.

  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup (recipe follows), if desired. Voila, easy French toast.

  4. Easy Spiced Syrup: Add 1 teaspoon vannila extract and 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon to 1 cup pancake syrup; stir well to mix.
